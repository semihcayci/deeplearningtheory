Supplementary References

List of references for basic machine learning.

A very good (accessible, clean, concise) reference for machine learning theory is:

  • Shai Shalev-Shwartz, and Shai Ben-David, "Understanding machine learning: From theory to algorithms," Cambridge University Press, 2014. (available online at https://www.cs.huji.ac.il/~shais/UnderstandingMachineLearning/understanding-machine-learning-theory-algorithms.pdf

I also recommend the lecture notes "Learning Theory from First Principles" by Francis Bach: https://www.di.ens.fr/%7Efbach/ltfp_book.pdf


For a completely rigorous treatment of machine learning, please refer to:

  • Ingo Steinwart, and Andreas Christmann, "Support vector machines," Springer Science & Business Media, 2008.
